Cherry Pie Recipe

What's The Deal With PIEEEEEEE???

We come together today to make THE MOST DELICIOUS cherry pie known to mankind, and I know that together we have the capacity for success. We are setting off to have the idea of a pie bakeable as it is, yet only in our own minds. We are doing this only to exercise skills unrelated and foreign to the world of pie baking. Yet here we are nonetheless a pie in hearts and minds. This pie baked on the grounds of knowledge, and only in our heads. So hear me now! No tongue shall grace hereby it's bounds with succulent crimson delight.
